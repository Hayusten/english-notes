Tap Listen to hear the pronunciation
Modify v.
To change something slightly to improve it.
“They modified the plan after the first review.”
🇵🇹 Modificar
Notify v.
To formally inform someone of something.
“Please notify the team about the schedule change.”
🇵🇹 Notificar
Outweigh v.
To be greater in importance than something else.
“The benefits clearly outweigh the risks.”
🇵🇹 Superar em importância
Permeate v.
To spread through and affect every part of something.
“A sense of calm permeated the room.”
🇵🇹 Permear, impregnar
Quell v.
To bring an end to something, often by effort.
“The manager quelled the team's concerns quickly.”
🇵🇹 Suprimir, acalmar
Reinforce v.
To strengthen or support something further.
“Practice reinforces what you learn in class.”
🇵🇹 Reforçar
